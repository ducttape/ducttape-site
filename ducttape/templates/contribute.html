Contribute

Why contribute?

The Ducttape Project is a free and open-source project developed by volunteers in their spare time. All those involved develop it for fun and learning, not for commercial prospects. As such, it needs contributors that are willing to invest their time and knowledge as well as abilities to move this project forward. Thus, if you want to help us, that would be awesome!

How to help?

If you are a programmer willing to help out, great! Please see below. Of course, programmers are very valuable people in a project of this nature but even if you can't program you can still try to download our files and see if they work on your system and give us feedback.
